Everything about plans, trials, payments, switching, and cancelling. You manage all of it from the **Billing** page in your account.

## Plans

- **Solo** — 1 property. $15/month or $149/year.
- **Portfolio** — up to 10 properties (plus overage beyond that). $39/month or $389/year.
- **Investor** — up to 50 properties. $169/month or $1,690/year.
- **Enterprise** — more than 50 properties. Contact sales.

Annual billing works out to roughly 17% less than paying monthly. Portfolio plans that exceed 10 properties add a small per-property charge (currently $5/month or $50/year each); see [Setting up multiple properties](/help/multi-property-setup).

## Your free trial

Every paid plan starts with a **14-day free trial**. A payment method is required to begin, but **you are not charged until day 15** — and if you cancel any time before then, you pay nothing. During the trial you have full use of the dashboard, financials, bookings, and period-over-period comparisons. The **Schedule E PDF and Excel exports** on the Reports page unlock once your plan becomes active.

## Changing your plan

You can switch tiers or move between monthly and annual at any time from the **Billing** page — open **Manage subscription** to make the change.

A couple of things to expect when you switch:

- **No proration.** Switching plans doesn't generate a mid-cycle charge or credit for the time you've already paid. Your new plan's price simply applies going forward.
- **Upgrades that change your billing term take effect immediately.** Because there's no proration, moving from monthly to annual (or otherwise changing your billing interval) starts the new term right away and charges the new term's price at that point.

If you upgrade from monthly to annual partway through a paid month and would like the unused portion of that month credited back, **just email us** — we handle those refunds manually on request. (See the cancellation note below for how this works in practice.)

## Cancelling

You can cancel any time from **Billing → Manage subscription**.

- **Your access continues until the end of the period you've already paid for.** Cancelling doesn't cut you off immediately; you keep full access through your current billing period, and you won't be charged again.
- After that date, the account locks but **your data is preserved** — nothing is deleted. Resubscribe whenever you like to pick up where you left off.
- We don't automatically prorate or refund the unused part of a period on cancellation. If your situation warrants a refund, **email support** and we'll sort it out.

## Payments and receipts

Payments are processed securely through Stripe; Hostics never stores your full card details. You can update your card from **Billing → Manage subscription**. Receipts for each charge are emailed to your billing address.

If you have a billing question this page doesn't answer, the support team is one click away at the bottom of every help page.
