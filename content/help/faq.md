Quick answers to the questions we hear most. For more detail, follow the links into the full guides.

### What is Hostics?

An analytics dashboard for short-term rental owners. You import your bookings and expenses, and Hostics shows your income, expenses, profit (NOI), and booking trends — then generates tax-ready, Schedule E–formatted reports. [Get started](/help/getting-started).

### Which platforms can I import from?

Airbnb, VRBO, Hostaway, and any CSV or Excel file with recognizable columns — including your own spreadsheet. [See importing guides](/help/importing-data).

### Do I need a credit card for the free trial?

Yes. Every plan starts with a 14-day trial that requires a card, but **you're not charged until day 15**, and cancelling before then costs nothing.

### What can I do during the trial?

Everything except exporting. The dashboard, financials, bookings, and period-over-period comparisons are all fully available during the trial. The **Schedule E PDF and Excel exports** unlock when your plan becomes active.

### What happens when I cancel?

You keep full access until the end of the period you've already paid for, and you won't be charged again. After that the account locks, but **your data is preserved** — you can resubscribe any time to pick up where you left off. [More on billing](/help/billing-and-subscriptions).

### If I switch from monthly to annual mid-period, do I get a refund for the unused month?

Hostics doesn't prorate automatically — switching plans applies the new price going forward with no automatic mid-cycle credit. If you'd like the unused portion of your monthly period refunded after switching to annual, **email support** and we'll handle it manually.

### Does Hostics charge me extra for more properties?

Solo covers 1 property and Investor covers up to 50. Portfolio includes 10; beyond that, each additional property adds a small charge (currently $5/month or $50/year), which you confirm when you add the property. [Plan details](/help/billing-and-subscriptions).

### Why is my revenue lower than the total my guests paid?

Hostics uses your **owner payout** — what you actually receive after platform and management fees — as revenue, rather than the gross booking amount. This keeps your profit figures honest. [How metrics are calculated](/help/understanding-metrics).

### Does Hostics show occupancy rate?

Yes — your **Dashboard** shows an occupancy rate (booked nights ÷ available nights) alongside average daily rate. One caveat: occupancy currently assumes each property was available for the entire date range, so a seasonal or recently-acquired property can read lower than its true figure. [How metrics are calculated](/help/understanding-booking-metrics).

### Is my data secure?

Payments are handled by Stripe and Hostics never stores your full card details. Your account data is private to you. If you cancel, your data is retained so you can return — it isn't deleted on cancellation.

### How do I reset my password?

From the sign-in page, click **Forgot password?**. One thing to know: the reset link must be opened in the same browser you requested it from. [Full steps](/help/password-reset).
