title: "Adding and Editing Properties"
category: "Managing Properties"

A property is the unit everything in Hostics hangs off — your bookings, expenses, and reports all roll up by property. Here's how to add, change, and remove them.

## Adding a property

1. Go to **Properties → Add Property**.
2. Enter at least a **name** and **address**. You can also record bedrooms, bathrooms, and property value, but those are optional.
3. Save.

> **Choose the name carefully.** When you import a file that covers multiple properties, Hostics matches each row to a property by this exact name. Keeping it consistent with what your booking and expense files call the property saves you a mapping step later. See [Importing multiple properties at once](/help/multi-property-import).

After you save an address, Hostics looks up its map coordinates in the background. If that doesn't happen right away, you can open the property in edit mode and use **Re-geocode**.

## Editing a property

1. Open **Properties** and click the property you want to change.
2. Update any field and click **Save Changes**.

Editing a property's details doesn't touch its bookings or expenses — those stay linked by the property itself, not by its name, so renaming a property won't orphan its existing data.

## Deleting a property

Open the property in edit mode and use **Delete Property** at the bottom. A confirmation explains exactly what will happen, because deletion in Hostics is deliberately gentle:

- The property is **hidden** from your dashboards and from the reservation/expense entry forms.
- Its historical data **stays in your reports**, so analytics and tax exports that span the period before deletion remain accurate and complete.
- This keeps a deleted property from silently erasing a year of bookings — but it also means deletion isn't reversible from inside the app. If you remove one by mistake, contact support.

If you're on the **Portfolio** plan and have more than 10 properties, deleting one that's over the included limit also reduces your overage at the next renewal — the confirmation dialog tells you the exact amount you'll save.

## Property limits by plan

- **Solo** — 1 property.
- **Portfolio** — up to 10 included. Adding an 11th (or beyond) prompts you to confirm a small per-property charge (currently $5/month or $50/year each), billed from your next renewal.
- **Investor** — up to 50 properties.
- **Enterprise** — more than 50; contact sales.

See [Billing & Account](/help/billing-account) for the full plan details.
