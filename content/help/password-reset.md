Forgot your password, or want to change it? Here's how to reset it.

## Request a reset link

1. Go to the **sign-in page** and click **Forgot password?**
2. Enter your email address and submit.
3. If an account exists for that address, you'll receive an email with a reset link. For your security, the confirmation message looks the same whether or not an account was found.

## Set your new password

1. Open the email and click the reset link.
2. On the page that opens, enter a new password (at least **8 characters**) and confirm it.
3. Submit — you'll be signed in and taken to your dashboard automatically.

## Important: open the link in the same browser

For security, the reset link must be opened in the **same browser and on the same device** you used to request it. If you request the reset on your laptop and then open the email link on your phone, it will show **"Reset link invalid or expired"** — even though nothing is actually wrong.

If you hit that message:

- Open the link in the browser you started from, **or**
- Request a fresh reset link from the device you're currently on and complete the whole flow there.

## Other reasons a link won't work

- **It's expired.** Reset links are time-limited. If too long has passed, request a new one.
- **It's already been used.** Each link works once. Request a new one if you need to reset again.
