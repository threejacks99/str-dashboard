Hostics turns your booking and expense data into a clear picture of how your short-term rental is actually performing — income, expenses, profit, and tax-ready reports. Here's how to get up and running.

## The four steps

1. **Add a property.** Go to **Properties → Add Property** and enter your rental's name and address. The name you choose here matters: when you import data later, Hostics matches rows to properties by this exact name, so pick something you'll recognize on your booking and expense files.
2. **Import your data.** Open **Add Data** and upload a CSV or Excel file from Airbnb, VRBO, Hostaway, or your own spreadsheet. Hostics reads the columns automatically and pulls in your reservations and expenses. See [Importing Data](/help/importing-data) for platform-by-platform steps.
3. **Review your numbers.** Your **Dashboard** shows the headline figures. **Financials** breaks down profit and loss, and **Bookings** covers reservation trends. Use the date-range and property filters at the top of each page to focus in.
4. **Export at tax time.** When you're ready, **Reports** generates a Schedule E–formatted PDF and an Excel export you can hand to your accountant.

## Starting from scratch? Set up your tracking first

If you've been running your rental without tracking your numbers, the best thing you can do before importing is get your data into a clean, consistent shape. Most owners already have the **income** side handled — Airbnb, VRBO, and most platforms let you export a transaction or earnings report, and Hostics reads those directly.

The side that usually needs work is **expenses**. Cleaning fees, repairs, supplies, insurance, mortgage interest, property tax, software — these rarely live in one place, and they're what turns a revenue number into a real profit number. A simple spreadsheet is all you need to start.

To make this easy, we've put together a **starter tracking template** with the exact column headers Hostics expects, so your file imports with no manual mapping:

> **[Download the Hostics tracking template](/hostics-tracking-template.xlsx)** — a ready-to-use Excel workbook with an Income sheet and an Expenses sheet, pre-labeled with the columns Hostics reads.

Fill in a row per booking and a row per expense, save it, and upload it through **Add Data**. For details on what each column means and how to format dates and amounts, see [Setting up your expense tracking](/help/importing-expenses).
