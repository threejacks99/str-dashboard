---
title: Understanding Your Metrics
---

Hostics calculates a range of figures across your Dashboard, Financials, and Bookings pages. These guides explain how each one is computed, what it includes and excludes, and what to look for.

One principle runs through all of them: **revenue in Hostics means your owner payout** — the money you actually receive after platform and management fees — not the gross amount a guest paid. And two kinds of reservations are always excluded from revenue and booking counts: **cancelled** bookings and **personal/owner stays**. That keeps your numbers reflecting real, earned income.
